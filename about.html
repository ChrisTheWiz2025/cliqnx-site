About CliqNx

Built for follow-through.

CliqNx was created with a simple idea: reminders should not be passive. They should trigger action, create accountability, and ensure that important things are actually completed.

The shift from reminders to accountability

Most tools stop at sending a notification. CliqNx is designed around what happens after that moment — who received it, who ignored it, who acted on it, and whether the outcome was verified.

The platform connects triggers, delivery channels, escalation, proof, approval, and activity tracking into one structured flow.

Simple on the surface

Creating a reminder, assigning it, and completing it should feel fast and natural. CliqNx is built to stay clear and intuitive for everyday use.

Power underneath

Behind that simplicity sits a more advanced system: multi-channel delivery, escalation paths, checklist validation, proof of completion, approval workflows, and a full audit trail.

Where CliqNx fits

CliqNx supports personal routines, family coordination, and operational workflows across teams and organisations.

It can be used for maintenance checks, compliance reminders, service schedules, inspections, defect follow-up, and general task accountability.

CliqNx is not a GPS tracking, dashcam, or hardware telematics system. It sits above those tools, focusing on the actions that need to happen and ensuring they are completed and recorded.

The direction

CliqNx is evolving into a platform where tasks are not just assigned, but followed through — from trigger to completion, with visibility at every step.

The goal is simple: nothing important gets ignored, and everything that matters is accounted for.
