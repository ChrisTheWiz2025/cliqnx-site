Solutions

One accountability engine. Many ways to use it.

CliqNx helps people and teams trigger important actions, deliver them through the right channels, escalate when they are ignored, collect proof, require approval, and keep a clear activity trail.

Personal routines

For important reminders that need more than a simple phone alert.

  • Medication and health routines
  • Appointments and deadlines
  • Home maintenance and recurring checks
  • Push reminders with completion notes

Family and care

For shared responsibilities where someone needs visibility and follow-up.

  • Children routines and homework checks
  • Elderly care reminders
  • Parent or carer follow-up
  • Approval and escalation when needed

Business operations

For teams that need proof, accountability, and a record of what happened.

  • Daily operational checks
  • Maintenance and service reminders
  • Item inspections and issue follow-up
  • Escalation through email, SMS, and voice

Maintenance, checks, and compliance follow-up

CliqNx can support vehicle, equipment, site, and asset workflows without pretending to be a GPS tracking or dashcam system. Use it for the tasks that must be completed around those assets.

  • Daily vehicle or forklift checks
  • CVRT, NCT, insurance, and service reminders
  • Photo proof for inspections
  • Defect follow-up linked to items
  • Audit history for completed and missed checks

CliqNx does not provide live vehicle tracking, dashcam footage, or telematics hardware. It helps make sure required actions, checks, and follow-ups are completed and recorded.

Team accountability without the noise

Many tools stop at sending a notification. CliqNx is designed around what happens after that: who received it, who ignored it, who completed it, and whether proof or approval was required.

  • Workspace roles for owners, admins, managers, users, and guests
  • Checklist questions for structured completion
  • Escalations and overdue repeat
  • Completion proof with comments and photos
  • Approval workflows for controlled sign-off

From reminder to resolved

Trigger the task, deliver it, escalate it, prove it, approve it, and track it. That is the CliqNx flow.
