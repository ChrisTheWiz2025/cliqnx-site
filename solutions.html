Solutions

One accountability engine. Many ways to use it.

CliqNx helps people and teams trigger important actions, deliver them through the right channels, escalate when they are ignored, collect proof, require approval, and keep a clear activity trail.

Personal routines

For important reminders that need more than a simple alert.

  • Medication and health routines
  • Appointments and deadlines
  • Home maintenance and recurring checks
  • Push reminders with completion tracking

Family and care

For shared responsibilities that need visibility and follow-up.

  • Children routines and homework checks
  • Elderly care reminders
  • Parent or carer escalation
  • Approval-based completion

Business operations

For teams that need proof, accountability, and traceability.

  • Daily operational checks
  • Maintenance and service reminders
  • Item inspections and issue tracking
  • Escalation via email, SMS, and voice

Maintenance, checks, and compliance

CliqNx supports vehicle, equipment, and site workflows without replacing tracking or hardware systems. It ensures the actions around those assets are completed and recorded.

  • Daily vehicle or forklift checks
  • CVRT, NCT, insurance, and service reminders
  • Photo proof for inspections
  • Defect tracking linked to items
  • Audit history of completed and missed tasks

CliqNx does not provide GPS tracking, dashcams, or telematics. It ensures required actions are followed through.

Team accountability

CliqNx focuses on what happens after a task is triggered — whether it is completed, ignored, escalated, or approved.

  • Structured workspace roles
  • Checklist-driven execution
  • Escalation and overdue follow-up
  • Proof with comments and photos
  • Approval workflows

From trigger to completion

Create the task, deliver it, escalate it, prove it, approve it, and track it. That is the CliqNx workflow.
